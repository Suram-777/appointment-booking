CAMPS

Health talk and multi-specialty camp

Fortis Escorts Hospital, Amritsar in association with Lions Club (Amritsar Chapter) organized an awareness talk and multi-specialty camp on 22nd January at Hindu College, Dhab Khatikan. Specialists from Orthopaedics, Gynecology, and Internal Medicine spearheaded the camp that was witnessed participation from about 60 people.

Healthy Heart and joints camp

As a part of it community connect initiatives, Fortis Escorts Hospital, Amritsar in association with NishkamSewaSamiti organized health screening camp. Primarily focused on heart and joints, over 135 people took part in this camp and got advise from the specialists. Dr. Rajeev Mehra (Senior Consultant Internal Medicine), Dr. Mohit Arora (Consultant Orthopaedic Surgeon), Dr. Dinesh Kumar and Dr. Balraj (Physiotherapist) championed this screening camp.

General Health camp at pathankot

Fortis Escorts Hospital, Amritsar in association with Khatri Sabha, Pathankot organized a health camp for the people of Phathankot and neighboring areas on 20th January. The camp was championed by Dr. Gurpreet Singh Sabharwal and Dr. H P Singh – Consultant Cardiologist. The participants were screened for diabetes, blood pressure and heart ailments. The camp witnessed participation from over 100 people.

Free medical camp held in Patancheru

Health tests were conducted for 150 persons at the free medical camp organised by doctors of TRR General Hospital in Indresham village gram panchayat. Free medicines were distributed. Ten doctors and four lab technicians, ten nurses/staff were present. The tests included general, eye, dental.
